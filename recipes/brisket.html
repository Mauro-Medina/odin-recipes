Tender Brisket

Brisket - tender, tasty and so easy!

... and also very nutritious. A recipe that everyone loves!

Ingredients

  • Roasting bag
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 pounds beef brisket
  • 1 ¾ fluid ounces liquid smoke flavoring
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Recipe instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C).
  2. Coat the inside of an oven roasting bag with flour. Place brisket inside of bag.
  3. Pour liquid smoke over the brisket and sprinkle on garlic powder, dry onion soup mix, and ground black pepper.
  4. Seal bag.
  5. Using a fork, make two sets of holes in the top of the roasting bag.
  6. Lay bag in a broiling pan. Bake in a preheated oven for 6 to 8 hours.
